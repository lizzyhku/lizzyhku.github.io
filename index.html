About

I am a third-year master student at Tsinghua University, majoring in Computer Technology. Previously, I recieved B.Eng degree in Software Engineering from Xidian University in 2021. I was a research intern supervised by Jieming Zhu and at Tencent mentored by Junwei Pan and Ximei Wang.

My research directions are data mining and recommender systems. During my M.Sc., I mainly focused on industrial recommender systems. For example,

  • Efficient Candidate Matching

  • Multi-scenario/Multi-task/Multi-modal CTR Prediction

  • Re-ranking Based on User Behavior Sequence

Recently, I am interested in Large Language Models (LLMs) and Generative AI. If you would like to work with me, please kindly contact me.
